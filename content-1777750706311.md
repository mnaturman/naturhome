## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does it cost?−

Ohai offers a FREE version so you can start organizing your household right away, with core features such as reminders, meal planning and lists. Premium plans start at $9.99 per month and unlocks features like shared household calendars, family task coordination, household reminders, and tools that help families manage schedules, meals, and responsibilities together.

### Is my data safe and protected?+

Yes. Protecting your family's information is a top priority. Ohai only uses your data to power features like calendar organization, reminders, and task coordination, and your information is never sold or shared for advertising purposes. Thousands of families trust Ohai with sensitive details like school schedules, appointments, and personal reminders, so strong privacy protections are built into the product.

### Is there human support behind the AI?+

Yes. Ohai combines AI automation with real human support when needed. The AI handles everyday tasks automatically, but if something is complicated, like a detailed school schedule or document, human assistants can step in to review and organize the information so nothing important is missed. Premium users also have the option to schedule a walkthrough with a member of the Ohai team to help them get set up and make the most of the product.

### What type of calendars can it sync to?+

Ohai syncs with the calendars families already use, including Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar (iCal). Once connected, Ohai brings events from work, school, and personal calendars into one shared household view so families can easily coordinate schedules, track appointments, and avoid conflicts across multiple types of calendars.

### How does Ohai help with school emails and sports schedules?+

Ohai can scan emails, PDFs, photos, and documents such as school newsletters, team schedules, or camp calendars and identify important dates, deadlines, and activities. It then suggests helpful actions like adding events to the family calendar, creating reminders, or generating tasks so parents can quickly turn long emails and attachments into an organized household schedule.

### How can I contact Ohai if I have more questions?+

You can reach the Ohai team anytime at support@ohai.ai with questions, feedback, or help getting started.
